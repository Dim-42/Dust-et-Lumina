It's the thunder before the storm

这是暴风雨前的雷鸣

The final line of an old poem

旧诗的末行

The first note of a new song

新曲的起音

On the edge of sight

视野的尽头

Barren stars stretch forth a new path

荒芜的星海延展出新的道路

"All glories are destined to end"

“所有的辉煌都有倾颓的一天”

"Shall one sink with this fleeting glow…"

“是与这回光返照的繁荣一同沉没……”

When the stars shone

群星闪耀时

Did they ever light the dark side of the moon

可曾照亮月之暗面

I was told

曾有人告诉我

Justice was but a saying

正义不过一种说辞

They nurtured the cradle of vice

他们滋养了恶的摇篮

The price paid by another

代价却有他者偿还

"Or shall we forge a new ark that sails far"

“还是去铸造新的方舟”

— Now I know at last

——我已得到答案

"Where hope yet flickers, let me feed the flames."

“或许希望在这里尚存，那就让我为之再添一点柴薪。”

——档案录入【瑞杰娜·柯林斯】

A voyage history has seen a thousand times

一场历史已见证千万次的启航

The scalding torch of ideals flares anew

愚者手中

In the hands of fools too stubborn to let go

滚烫的理想淬火重燃

Do you hear it?

你听到了吗？
